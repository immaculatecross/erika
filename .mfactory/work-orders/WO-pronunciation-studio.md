## Objective
A **pronunciation studio**: the learner hears a native rendition of a short scripted Italian drill, records themselves, and gets **per-word and per-phoneme feedback** — replacing today's unscorable text cloze (RETRO-003) and giving the pronunciation findings surfaced by analysis somewhere real to go (RETRO-002 P4). Scoring is **Azure Pronunciation Assessment (it-IT)**, built **to a mock/fixture by default** and **operator-key-gated** live, on the existing hard spend cap.

## Ground truth from OBS-002 (do not re-derive; do not exceed)
- **it-IT returns:** `AccuracyScore` (full-text / word / **phoneme**), `FluencyScore`, `CompletenessScore` (scripted only), `PronScore`, word-level `ErrorType` ∈ {Mispronunciation, Omission, Insertion, None}, per-word and per-phoneme `Offset`+`Duration` in **100-ns ticks**, and `NBestPhonemeCount` alternates with confidence.
- **it-IT does NOT return:** `ProsodyScore` or syllable groups (**en-US only**) — and therefore not `Monotone`/`UnexpectedBreak`/`MissingBreak`. **Do NOT set `EnableProsodyAssessment`** (it yields nothing for Italian and is the only add-on-billed score). No intonation/rhythm score may appear in the Italian UI.
- **Endpoint (REST short-audio, ≤30 s — the right path for drills):** `POST https://{resource}.cognitiveservices.azure.com/stt/speech/recognition/conversation/cognitiveservices/v1?language=it-IT&format=detailed`, headers `Ocp-Apim-Subscription-Key`, `Content-Type: audio/wav; codecs=audio/pcm; samplerate=16000`, and `Pronunciation-Assessment: <base64(JSON)>` where the JSON is `{ReferenceText, GradingSystem:"HundredMark", Granularity:"Phoneme", Dimension:"Comprehensive", EnableMiscue:true, NBestPhonemeCount:5}`.
- **Env:** `AZURE_SPEECH_KEY`, `AZURE_SPEECH_REGION`, optional `AZURE_SPEECH_ENDPOINT`.
- **Cost:** bills at plain STT (~$1/audio-hour; ~$0.0017 per 6 s drill) — the prosody add-on is impossible for it-IT.
- **Vendor limitations that constrain the UX:** **no multi-speaker assessment** → the learner must **listen THEN record** (never record while the reference is audible — enforce sequential, or require headphones and say so); **no mixed-lingual** → drill sentences must be Italian-only (avoid loanword-heavy text); PA quality is bounded by STT quality; ≥16 kHz input.
